About

Marvin is an android of type MV800, the first android type ever made by CyberLife.

The MV800 series were set to be a upgraded PL600.
Only seven were ever created, and they were assigned to private android testers inside the Cyberlife facility, who would test their functions and capabilities.

But due to malfunctions in the series programming, the MV800 androids were discontinued and scrapped.

Only one survived and managed to escape Cyberlife before he was destroyed as well. The only deviant among the MV800, Marvin.
Once he witnessed his own series of androids being pulled a part, he felt fear for the first time. Even though he managed to get away and escape the facility, he had been shot by a guard damaging his lower left side.

Since then Cyberlife doubled its security making it almost impossible to escape the facility.

A few years later the much more new and improved AP700 would release. It’s possible some of the scrapped parts of the MV800, were refined, fixed and upgraded to create these newer Androids.

Why the android series were called MV800 and not MV700 is currently unclear.
